SurakshaDrishti AI

AI-Powered Real-Time Public Safety Surveillance System

SurakshaDrishti AI is a local real-time AI surveillance command-center prototype that combines live camera monitoring, YOLO object detection, person tracking, SOS emergency alerts, authority response workflow, heatmap analytics, and an AI assistant to support smarter public safety decisions.
